{{Quote|I am not forgotten, you know, no, I still receive a very great deal of fan mail... Gladys Gudgeon writes weekly... I just wish I knew why...|An amnesiac [[Gilderoy Lockhart]] about Gladys's continued admiration of him|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix}}
'''Gladys Gudgeon''' was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] and fan of [[Gilderoy Lockhart]].<ref name="COS7">{{COS|B|7}}</ref><ref name="OOTP23">{{OOTP|B|23}}</ref> She wrote fan letters to him every week,<ref name="COS7"/> and continued to do so when Lockhart was admitted to [[St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries]], even though Lockhart, having lost his memory, had no idea why she was writing to him.<ref name="OOTP23"/> 

==Etymology==
The name "Gladys" is from the old Welsh name ''Gwladus'', possibly derived from ''gwlad'' "country".
