'''Lorcan Scamander'''<ref>{{WU}}</ref> was one of the twins born to [[Luna Lovegood]] and [[Rolf Scamander]]. He has a twin brother named [[Lysander Scamander|Lysander]].<ref name="Family">{{JKYL}} {{see image|File:Weasley Family Tree.jpg}}</ref>

==Biography==
When his mother travelled to the [[Patagonian Desert]], in [[Argentina]], to attend the final of the [[2014 Quidditch World Cup|427th Quidditch World Cup]], in [[July]] [[2014]], Lorcan was left at home with his grandfather, along with [[Lysander Scamander|Lysander]].<ref name="dumbledores-army-reunites" />

==Etymology==
*Lorcan means "little fierce one" in the Irish language.<ref>{{Wikilink|Lorcan}}</ref>
