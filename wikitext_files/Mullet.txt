'''Mullet''' was an Irish [[wizardkind|witch]] who played as the [[Chaser]] for the [[Irish National Quidditch team]] during the [[1994 Quidditch World Cup]].

She played during the [[1994 Quidditch World Cup]] final against [[Bulgarian National Quidditch team|Bulgaria]], which the Irish team won. She won her side a penalty when Bulgarian [[Keeper]], [[Lev Zograf]] fouled her by [[cobbing]].<ref>{{GOF|B|8}}</ref>

==Etymology==
A "mullet" is a hairstyle and a kind of fish.
